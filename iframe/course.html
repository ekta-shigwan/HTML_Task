Course

NIIT offers training in diverse domains to graduates, which include courses after graduation. These diploma courses enable students to boost their career prospects and find placement in both IT and non-IT industries. The Diploma courses after graduation, available for many sunrise segments such as Digital and Finance and Business Analytics, help learners ‘future-proof’ themselves and remain relevant for the rapidly evolving technology marketplace. NIIT’s Job-oriented courses also help professionals to upgrade their skills and move up the corporate ladder. These career advancement programs set professionals on the path of continuous learning, helping them upgrade their skills on a consistent basis and build expertise in the technologies of tomorrow. At the same time, these Graduate Diploma courses enable learners to acquire skills in specific industry segments, positioning them as domain specialists in these areas.

NIIT offers courses in the following domains to graduates:

  • Information Technology
  • Banking & Finance
  • Digital Marketing
  • Business Analytics
  • Retail
  • Telecom
