About GET exam

  • The total time allotted in the written exam is 60 minutes.
  • There is no negative marking in the paper.
  • Aptitude section consists of questions from the topics like algebra, time & work, time, speed & distance, arithmetic, profit & loss, HCF, LCM, geometry, percentages, probability, permutation & combination, etc.
  • The aptitude section is of the moderate level and also includes the logical reasoning questions.
  • Technical section involved questions from Computer Programs, DS & Basic algorithms and C programming.
  • Coding section consists of questions from C language, DS, and Basic Algorithms. It is a paper-based exam.
  • Overall the level of the question paper is moderate to difficult.
  • Only those aspirants who clear the written exam will qualify for the next round.
  • The NIIT Technologies reserves the right to make changes in the written test.
